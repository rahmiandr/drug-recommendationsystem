For Central Nervous System

Welcome!

Drugs that act on the central nervous system based on their pharmacodynamic effects are divided into two major groups, namely stimulating and inhibiting certain processes in brain activity, spinal cord and nerves. The data used in the database is sourced from MIMS Indonesia.

Steps to use this website:
1. Check the data you want to search for in the brand drug database first. 
2. After finding the drug you want to search for, enter the drug name into the form using an uppercase prefix instead of lowercase letters. For example: Paraco not paraco.
3. Click submit to see drug recommendations.
